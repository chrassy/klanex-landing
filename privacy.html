Privacy note

How this website uses analytics. Last updated July 2026.

Analytics, only after you consent

klanexai.com uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how the site is used — which pages and referrers bring people in, so we can improve the site. Nothing loads and no data is sent until you click Accept on the consent banner. If you decline, no analytics run at all.

What is collected (after consent)

  • Standard web-analytics events: pages viewed, referrer/source, approximate location (country/region), device and browser type.
  • Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to measure repeat visits. IP addresses are anonymized (anonymize_ip).
  • Google processes this data as our analytics provider. See Google's privacy policy.

Withdrawing consent

Your choice is stored locally in your browser. To change it, clear this site's data (or the klanex_analytics_consent entry in local storage) and the banner will appear again. You can also block analytics with a browser extension or your browser's tracking-prevention settings.

The product is separate

This note covers the marketing website only. Data you send to the klanex API (executions, payloads, credentials) is governed by the product's terms and is never fed into website analytics. Credentials are encrypted at rest and never exposed in reads.
