Privacy note

How this website uses analytics. Last updated July 2026.

Cookieless analytics

klanexai.com uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how the site is used — which pages and referrers bring people in, so we can improve the site. It is privacy-first by design: no cookies, no localStorage, no fingerprinting, and no cross-site tracking. Because it stores nothing on your device and collects no personal data, there is no consent banner to click through.

What is collected

  • Aggregate, anonymous page metrics: pages viewed, referrer/source, and coarse device and country information — never tied to an individual or a persistent identifier.
  • No advertising profiles, no data sold or shared, nothing that follows you to other sites.
  • Cloudflare processes this as our analytics provider. See Cloudflare's Web Analytics and privacy policy.

Opting out

There is nothing to opt out of storage-wise, but you can block the analytics beacon with any browser extension or your browser's tracking-prevention settings if you prefer.

The product is separate

This note covers the marketing website only. Data you send to the klanex API (executions, payloads, credentials) is governed by the product's terms and is never fed into website analytics. Credentials are encrypted at rest and never exposed in reads.
